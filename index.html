## 01 · whoami

About

I'm an infrastructure and product engineer based in Dubai. My work lives where backend code meets the platform it runs on: CI/CD pipelines, AWS infrastructure, and the API contracts that let frontend and backend teams ship in parallel instead of waiting on each other.

Currently Backend Engineering Lead at Hightekers. Before that: Mozn, on the platform behind AI risk products; three and a half years at Kaso (YC W22), where I led the platform team for B2B food-supply commerce; and HIPAA-compliant health-tech at Medicus AI. Along the way I've automated release pipelines, cut build times, reverse-engineered undocumented data formats, and turned "works on my machine" into "runs in production".

I care about boring reliability: pipelines that never surprise you, APIs that mean what the spec says, and infrastructure you can hand to the next engineer without an apology.
